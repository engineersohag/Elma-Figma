Recalls & Product Safety Alerts

Emela Company Limited

Emela Company Limited prioritizes consumer safety. We are committed to offering products that comply with applicable safety standards and laws. This policy explains how we manage recalls and safety alerts and how we communicate important information to customers.

Our Commitment to Product Safety

Emela Company Limited collaborates with sellers and regulatory authorities to ensure products on our platform are safe.

What Is a Product Recall or Safety Alert

A product recall or safety alert may be found to pose a risk if it:

  • Poses a risk to health or safety
  • Fails to meet legal or regulatory requirements
  • Contains serious defects or misleading labeling or usage instructions

How We Notify Customers

Depending on the situation, Emela may notify customers via the platform regarding:

  • Full or partial refunds
  • Follow return, repair, replacement, or disposal guidance
  • Contacting customer support if assistance is needed

Customer Actions and Replacements

Depending on the situation, Emela may result in clear instructions for the steps of the items.

Returns, Refunds, and Replacements

Depending on the situation, Emela may offer replacements or repairs promptly.

  • Full or partial refunds, replacement products
  • Repairs or corrective measures in the recall or safety

Reporting a Safety Concern

If you believe a product purchase from Emela may be unsafe, please contact our customer support team with:

  • Product name and order details
  • Description of the safety issue
  • Relevant photos or supporting information
