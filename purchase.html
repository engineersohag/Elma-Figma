Purchase Protection

Emela Company Limited

Emela is dedicated to providing a secure and trustworthy shopping experience. Our Purchase Protection Policy helps protect buyers if something goes wrong with their order.

1

What This Policy Covers

Depending on verification, buyers may be eligible for a replacement or store credit for:

  • Item not received
  • Item received damaged or defective
  • Item significantly different from the description
  • Incorrect item delivery

Depending on verification, buyers may be eligible for a refund, replacement, or store credit.

2

Eligibility Requirements

  • Orders must be completed and paid through Emela
  • Claims must be submitted within 7 days of delivery or scheduled delivery date
  • Supporting evidence such as photos, videos, or order details may be required

Emela does not support or protect transactions conducted out.

3

Items Not Covered

  • Damage caused by misuse, negligence, or normal wear and tear
  • Digital products after access or download
  • Perishable items after delivery
  • Items marked as non-returnable or final sale

Orders completed outside the Emela platform.

4

How to File a Claim

  • Go to My Orders in your Emela account
  • Select the relevant order
  • Click Report a Problem
  • Submit required details and supporting evidence
5

Resolution Process

  • Issue a full or partial refund
  • Arrange a replacement
  • Provide store credit
  • Reject the claim if policy conditions are not met

Refund Method

  • Approved refunds are processed through the original payment method or the Emela wallet.
  • Processing times may vary by payment provider.

Buyer Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate delivery information
  • inspect items upon delivery
  • Report issues honestly and promptly.
